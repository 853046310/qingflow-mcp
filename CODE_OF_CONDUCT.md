# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a welcoming and respectful environment for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

1. Be respectful and constructive in communication.
2. Assume good intent; discuss ideas, not people.
3. Provide actionable technical feedback.

## Unacceptable Behavior

1. Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
2. Publishing private information without permission.
3. Abusive or threatening language.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, issues, or contributions that violate this policy.
Repeated or severe violations may result in temporary or permanent bans from project spaces.
